## Concept explanation
Governance sets approvals, risk reviews, and compliance checks. Incentives ensure teams care about safety and learning, not just speed.

### Failure mode focus
- Incentives misaligned. Fix by adding safety KPIs.
- Shadow changes. Fix with approvals and audit logs.

<Callout type="info" title="Concept">
UI communicates calibration. Show confidence, evidence, and alternatives. Require confirmation for risky actions. Avoid greenwashing that inflates trust.
</Callout>

### Failure mode focus
- Over-trust from green badges. Fix with calibrated bars + warnings.
- Under-trust slows work. Fix with explanations of safeguards.
